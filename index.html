Julia Khusainova

San Francisco, CA

Julia is currently employed as a Senior Product Designer at Shyp. Previously she worked at Twitter and led product design at early stage start-ups including 23Snaps, Roadacious, and Versal.

Julia is recognized as a travel and lifestyle photographer. Her works have been featured in many publications including Saveur, Sweet Dreams and NetMagazine.
